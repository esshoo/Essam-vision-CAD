v37 - VR lights movement refresh fix

What changed:
- Real lights in VR now refresh automatically while the user moves.
- Selection uses the dolly/player position instead of only the camera head position.
- Refresh is throttled and movement-based to avoid lag.
- VR runtime budget for real lights was relaxed slightly:
  - more lights can stay active in VR
  - density is higher than before
  - still capped conservatively for performance

Expected result:
- Lights update while walking/teleporting inside VR
- No need to exit VR or touch settings to refresh active lights
- More than 1-2 lights can stay active when the file and slider level allow it
